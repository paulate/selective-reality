Selective Reality

Featuring

We are losing the ability to focus in this world of a billion sensors and constant information overload. Selective Reality is a philosophical framework that challenges popular notions of augmented reality. Within this framework, a range of scenarios for physical devices provoke real applications for the present and future. By empowering people to take control of their perceptions, we achieve a greater focus on the people we love, the things we enjoy, and the work that matters.
